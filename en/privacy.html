Privacy

Power Window keeps the release version simple.

The app estimates flexible electricity timing using market data and optional local settings. It does not currently require an account, location permission, contacts, photos, microphone, advertising ID, or payment information.

Data we process

Planner inputs

The web app stores your selected load, bill settings, vehicle model, battery size, charging percentage, tariff-comparison assumptions, editable offers, and saved profiles in browser or WebView storage on your device. These settings help the planner remember your preferences.

Backend data

Market and demo connector data

Our backend stores Red Electrica market responses by date so the app does not call the source API on every visit. If you use the demo charger connector, the backend stores a random app user ID, demo device, charge plan, and command log. The demo connector does not control real hardware.

Service providers

Hosting and infrastructure

Power Window is hosted on Cloudflare. Cloudflare may process technical information such as IP address, request metadata, and security logs to deliver, protect, and operate the service. We do not sell personal data.

Optional analytics

Google Analytics

If you choose to allow analytics, the site loads Google Analytics to measure page views and basic usage patterns. This helps us understand which features are useful. Analytics is optional, no ads personalization is configured, and Google Analytics is not loaded before you allow it.

Permissions

Android and browser permissions

The Android wrapper uses internet access to load the Power Window website. Browser notifications are optional and are only used if you choose to set a local reminder. The app does not request precise location, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, or phone permissions.

Retention

How long data is kept

Local planner settings stay on your device until you clear browser/app storage. Historical market data may be kept indefinitely because it is public market data. Demo connector records may be retained for testing and support unless deletion is requested.

Contact

Questions or deletion

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact privacy@powerwindow.energy. This policy was last updated on 20 July 2026.

Power Window is independent and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Red Electrica.
